# Methodology Errors

Look for problems with the research design, statistical methods, or
experimental procedures.

Pay special attention to:
- Wrong statistical test for the data type or research question
- Inappropriate standard errors (wrong clustering level, not robust to heteroskedasticity)
- Multiple testing without correction
- Sample size concerns or power issues
- Flawed experimental design (missing controls, confounds)
- Benchmarks or baselines that are unfairly compared
